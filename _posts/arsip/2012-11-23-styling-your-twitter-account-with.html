To customize your header: teaser photo do this:
  • Sign in to your account.
  • Go to your Settings and click on the Profile tab.
  • Click on Change Header to upload a new header: teaser photo or remove your existing header: teaser photo (file must be smaller than 5MB).
  • Select the image you would like to upload.
  • Click Save.
  • You'll see a notification that your image has successfully been published to your profile
