Setidaknya untuk bulan april, ada 7 drama korea yang akan dirilis. Di bulan april 2014 ini beberapa drama yang rilis maret 2014 masih ada yang di tayangkan seperti 3 Days, God Gift 14 Days, Sly and Single Again, Red Sun dan tentu saja drama panjang Wonderfull Days

Sekarang akan saya berikan informasi singkat 7 Drama Korea Pilihan Tayang April 2014 untuk bahan referensi drama yang bisa anda pilih untuk di tonton di bulan April 2014. Bookmark halaman ini, karena akan di update setiap ada rilis dan informasi baru berkaitan dengan drama pilihan terbaru tahun 2014 ini

1. Angel Eyes



Angel Eyes, Release Date: April 5, 2014

English Synopsis
Park Dong-Joo is a surgeon. His first love was a blind girl. Yoon Soo-Wan is an emergency 119 worker. She was blind when she was younger, but an eye transplant surgery allowed her to see.
Park Dong-Joo and Yoon Soo-Wan were each other's first love. They separated due to sad family histories. They meet again 12 years later.

Selengkapnya:
Angel Eyes Episode 01 - Episode 20

2. Hotel King



Hotel King, Release Date: April 5, 2014

English Synopsis
Cha Jae-Wan (Lee Dong-Wook) and his mother were abandoned by his father. As an adult, he begins to work at a hotel and stands up against his father.

Selengkapnya:
"Hotel King" Episode 01 - Episode 32

3. Big Man


Release Date: April 14, 2014

English Synopsis
Kim Ji-Hyeok (Kang Ji-Hwan) doesn't have parents and goes through a tough life. He then meets Dal-Sook who runs a small restaurant at a traditional market. Kim Ji-Hyeok follows her like his mother and settles down at the traditional market. He works hard to open up his own store within the traditional market.
Suddenly, Kim Ji-Hyeok becomes a hidden son from a family that owns Korea's top company Hyunsung. He realizes though that there are impure intentions. Since that moment, Kim Ji-Hyeok takes revenge upon the people at the Hyunsung company. He becomes romantically involved with a woman named So Mi-Ra (Lee Da-Hee) who he meets at the most important time in his life.

5. A New Leaf


Release Date: April 23, 2014


English Synopsis

6. Doctor Stranger


Release Date: April 28, 2014

English Synopsis
As a child, Park Hoon (Lee Jong-Suk) and his father were kidnapped by North Korea. In North Korea, Park Hoon was trained to become a doctor by his father who was already a doctor. Park Hoon became a genius like chest surgeon. He then flees to South Korea.
Park Hoon begins to work as a doctor in South Korea's top hospital Dongwoo University Hospital, but he feels like a complete outsider. To bring his love from North Korea, he does anything to make money.

7. You're All Surrounded


Relaease Date: April 30, 2014

English Synopsis
"You're All Surrounded" follows a twenty something year old rookie detective (Lee Seung-Gi) at a police station in Gangnam, Seoul, South Korea.
