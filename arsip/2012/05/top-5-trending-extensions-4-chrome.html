4. Better Music for Google Play Music

Better Music for Google Play Music
Deskripsi dari developer:

A Chrome extension for Google Music to improve the experience: scrobbling, toast notifications, play controls, and more!
This is for the change of Google Music's domain from music.google.com to play.google.com/music.
Better Music is a tool to help get more out of Google Play Music. It gives you controls to play/pause, skip, reverse, shuffle, and repeat songs in any Chrome window. It also has toast notifications of song change and scrobbling! More to come.
Features
  • Scrobbling of songs (option to turn on or off)
  • Ability to "love" the song on Last.FM
  • Google thumbs up/down
  • Desktop notifications (toasts) of song changes (option to turn off)
  • Playback controls (Play/Pause, Skip, Reverse, Shuffle, and Repeat) in a button on the browser bar
  • Persistent "miniplayer" in options panel (notifications still happen unless toggled off)
  • Playlist switching (autoplay on switch) in minplayer and popup (browser button) windows

5. YouTube Options for Google Chrome™

YouTube Options for Google Chrome™
Deskripsi dari developer:


Disable annoying things in YouTube and other video sites.

YouTube Options: disable ads, annotations, and auto-play; change resolution, display size, optional flash pre-buffering, looping/replay, SSL; download all available versions of the media (this will require installing the full version from the developers website).

Flash videos are pre-buffered by default when using the disable auto-play option.  If you dont like pre-buffering, there is an option to turn it off.

Set desired display size or resolution, automatically sync the display size to the resolution, or expand video to the size of window.

Page modifications include the ability to disable everything on the page except for the video (header: image, video title, description, user comments, suggested videos, footer, playlist, branding) and matting the background.

Use the page action popup menu to hide everyone on the page with one click, or parts of the page with multiple clicks, turn looping on or off, or adjust the display size on-the-fly.

Open suggested videos in a new window, a chromeless popout window!

Most (not all) features also work with Vimeo, Dailymotion, Metacafe, G4TV, FEARnet, Funny or Die, Hulu, The Escapist, and the Dump.

Catatan saya: Youtube"s Best Extensions 4 Chrome yang membuat semakin nyaman ketika melihat konten video di youtube dengan membuang hal-hal yang (sebagian besar)tidak kita perlukan untuk di lihat (kita ke youtube untuk nonton film, bukan iklan).

Link Extension: http://goo.gl/yxOm5
Version: 1.8.70
Updated: Wednesday, April 18, 2012
Language: English
